Findings

Two markets, one city

Independent houses list at roughly 3× the median price of flats, on much larger plots — switch the segment toggle above and watch every chart move.

Location is a step function

Median prices across sectors span about 5×. The golden-corridor sectors top the grid; peripheral sectors stay affordable.

3 BHK is the centre of gravity

The single largest supply segment. Median price roughly doubles with each bedroom added, up to 5 BHK.

Old ≠ premium: a Simpson’s paradox

In aggregate, old properties show the highest ₹/sq.ft. — but 58% of old listings are houses, which carry ~2.5× the ₹/sq.ft. of flats. Within flats, under-construction stock is priciest.

Data quality decided the analysis

126 duplicate listings and ~1% unit-error records (an “875,000 sq.ft. flat”) were removed by a documented pipeline before any statistic was trusted.
